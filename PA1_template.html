making histogram steps by day. at first look it looks like normal distribution just with more thick first half

hist(aggregate(steps ~ date, data = data, sum)$steps, main = "Histogram of steps by day",
     xlab = "number of steps")

here we can look at histogram more closely, using “breaks”, and we see something interseting - days with extra activity (more than 20000 steps)

hist(aggregate(steps ~ date, data = data, sum)$steps, breaks = 10, main = "Histogram of steps by day",
     xlab = "number of steps")
